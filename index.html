Who We Are

More Than A Charity.
We Are A Movement.

Misiunea Iubirii (Mission of Love) is not just an organization; it is a promise kept. Founded in the United States with deep operational roots in Eastern Europe, we exist to bridge the gap between those who have resources and those who have lost all hope.

We believe that love is a verb. It is not enough to feel bad for the trafficked child or the starving widow; we must act. Our team operates with military precision and Christ-like compassion, cutting through red tape to deliver aid where it is needed now.

Success Story

From Darkness to Daughter.

In one of the darkest corners of Romania, Ana and Irina were more than just statistics in the 10,000+ victims of trafficking detected annually. They were children, stolen and hopeless.

Because of donors like you, Misiunea Iubirii intervened. Today, they aren't just safe; they are restored. They have warm beds, school supplies, and the knowledge that they are daughters of the King.
