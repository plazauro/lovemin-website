Gaza: Winter Storm & Flooding

Status: Rescue & Recovery Operations Ongoing

Date: December 13–15, 2025
Casualties: 14+ confirmed dead (including children)
Primary Threat: Structural collapse & Hypothermia

A severe winter storm bringing heavy rainfall, freezing temperatures, and strong winds has caused widespread devastation in the Gaza Strip. Already vulnerable infrastructure has failed, leading to building collapses and massive flooding.

Families are exposed to freezing conditions without shelter. Immediate humanitarian aid is required to prevent further loss of life from exposure.

Misiunea Iubirii Response:

  • Emergency Shelter: Providing thermal tents and blankets to displaced families.
  • Food & Heat: Distributing hot meals and portable heaters.
  • Medical Support: Assisting local partners with triage for hypothermia cases.

Our Relief Strategy

We don't just send money. We send teams. Our rapid response model is built on three phases of intervention.

PHASE 1

Rapid Assessment

Within 24-48 hours of a catastrophe, our local partners and scout teams are on the ground. We assess critical needs—identifying where the most vulnerable (elderly, children) are trapped.

PHASE 2

Immediate Relief

We deploy the "Survival Packs." Food, clean water, hygiene kits, and emergency blankets are distributed directly to families. We cut through red tape to feed people now.

PHASE 3

Rebuild & Restore

After the cameras leave, we stay. We help families clear mud from homes, repair roofs, and replace essential furniture so they can return to dignity.
