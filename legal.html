Our Legal Services

We partner with experienced immigration lawyers to provide pro-bono and subsidized legal aid.

Paperwork & Filing

The immigration system is buried in bureaucracy. We help families file the correct forms for visas, residency, and asylum to prevent rejections based on technicalities.

I.C.E. Intervention

When someone is detained, every hour matters. We work to locate detained family members, connect them with legal representation, and fight for their release on bond.

Family Reunification

Our ultimate goal is keeping families together. We provide legal strategies for reunification, whether navigating the visa process or fighting deportation orders.

Success Story

"They told me I would never see my children again."

Maria* was detained during a routine check. With limited English and no lawyer, she was facing immediate deportation. Her three children were left terrified with a neighbor.

Misiunea Iubirii stepped in. We secured an immigration attorney who paused the deportation, filed the correct asylum paperwork, and most importantly, brought Maria home to her children while her case is processed.

*Name changed for protection.
